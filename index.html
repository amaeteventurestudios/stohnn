About

A DIFFERENT
CATEGORY.

Stohnn does not improve on conventional HVAC. It replaces the architecture that every conventional system is built on. The result is a thermal management system with fundamentally fewer failure modes, lower operating costs, and no dependence on combustion or software for core operation.

THE COMPRESSOR IS THE PROBLEM.
WE REMOVED IT.
∅
No Moving Parts in the Thermal Loop

The fluid circulation system has no rotating or reciprocating components. There is nothing to seize, nothing to lubricate in the fluid circuit, and nothing to generate vibration. The pump that moves coolant through the system operates on electric fields alone.

⚡
Fully Electrified

No combustion anywhere in the system. Stohnn meets federal building electrification mandates by design, not by retrofit. Federal buildings are required to eliminate fossil fuel use for new construction and major renovation by 2030. Stohnn is already there.

◎
Passive Mechanical Intelligence

Flow regulation responds to the physics of the system, not to a software command. No control loops that can drift. No firmware that can be exploited. The system regulates itself correctly because it is engineered to do so mechanically.

▲
Space-Validated Architecture

The core fluid circulation technology was validated in continuous operation for two years in one of the most demanding environments that exists. That record is not a marketing claim. It is an operational data point from an environment where failure is not an option.

System 01
Stohnn Solid-State Pump Array

Electric fields move dielectric coolant through a sealed thermal loop. No moving parts. No compressor. No rotating components in the fluid circuit. The pump array is modular. Capacity scales by adding modules in parallel for greater flow or in series for longer pumping distance. Nothing to seize, nothing to lubricate, nothing to fail in the conventional mechanical sense. Validated in continuous operation for two years in one of the most demanding environments that exists.

System 02
Stohnn Thermal Core

Graphite mini-channel design replacing conventional metal heat exchangers. Graphite delivers a higher ratio of thermal conductivity to density than metal, expands less under heat cycling, and produces significantly less flow resistance. Demonstrated pressure drop of 0.066 psi at 1,100 watts of thermal transport, compared to 1 to 5 psi typical of metal alternatives. That is 15 to 75 times lower resistance. In facilities running cooling continuously, lower pumping resistance compounds into significant annual energy savings.

System 03
Stohnn Flow Regulation Valve

Passive, proportional, bidirectional flow control. As pressure builds on one side of the valve, the reed opens proportionally to demand. When pressure drops, it closes. No motor. No control signal. No software. The system regulates itself in response to the physics of what is happening in the thermal loop. This is what Stohnn means by passive mechanical intelligence. A conventional system uses a motorized actuator or software loop that can drift, fail, or be exploited. Stohnn uses mechanics.

System 04
Stohnn Axial Fan

Non-linear blade twist redistributes aerodynamic load away from the blade tip, dissipating the tip vortex over a wider area. Tip loading is where conventional fans waste the most energy and generate the most noise. More than 15 percent improvement in power consumption at equivalent thrust compared to conventional blade designs, with significantly less aerodynamic noise. Blade diameter and rotation speed remain unchanged. The efficiency comes from geometry.

System 05
Inlet and Duct Acoustic Liner

Coupled resonator design reduces duct noise transmission without airflow penalty. Multiple resonators tuned to different frequencies share a single inlet, reducing the number of facesheet openings by a factor of two or more without degrading attenuation. Less open area means less drag. Stohnn has four independent acoustic control layers: the bearing assembly eliminates shaft wear noise, the fan blade geometry reduces tip noise, the duct liner reduces transmission noise, and the absence of a compressor removes the loudest single component in any conventional system.

System 06
Stohnn Precision Bearing Assembly

Corrosion-proof, shock-resistant nickel-titanium alloy at every rotating component. Immune to rust. Does not chemically degrade lubricants. 15 percent lighter than steel. The small-diameter bearings in fan motors and actuators use an advanced hafnium alloy variant achieving Rockwell hardness of HRC 58 to 60 with zero measurable wear after 10,000 hours of continuous testing. These are not incremental improvements on steel bearings. They are a different material category built for long-duration continuous operation.

System 07
Stohnn Multi-Stage Air Filter

Self-regenerating impactor pre-filter and scroll filter mechanism. The impactor surface cleans itself automatically on a continuous basis. The scroll mechanism advances clean filter media as sections load. Conventional pre-filter systems require cleaning or replacement as often as once per week in high-particulate environments. Stohnn's system extends service intervals dramatically with no manual intervention required between scheduled maintenance cycles. Validated in extreme environments including closed-system, high-pressure, high-particulate conditions.

Mission

BUILT FROM SIGNALS
THE MARKET HAS NOT READ.

1920 × 600
mission-hero.jpg
Aerospace research facility interior. High ceilings. Precision equipment. Blue-white lighting. No people.

The technology behind Stohnn systems has existed for years. It was developed and validated by researchers working on some of the most demanding thermal management problems in existence, including continuous operation aboard the International Space Station. The core fluid circulation technology ran without failure for two years in that environment.

It was never commercialized. Not because it does not work. Because the organizations that could have acted on it had no incentive to. Every major HVAC manufacturer is optimized around a compressor-based architecture. A technology that eliminates the compressor is not an improvement to their product. It is a replacement for it. That is a different kind of threat, and large incumbents do not move toward existential threats voluntarily.

The scaling question also stopped early evaluators. The technology was demonstrated at component scale. Nobody funded the engineering work to answer whether it could scale to building-level thermal loads. That answer is what Stohnn exists to find and to build around.

800 × 600
mission-space.jpg
International Space Station exterior. Black space. Earth below. Solar panels extended.

The federal electrification mandate created the procurement urgency that did not exist before. Federal buildings must be fully electrified by 2030. That is a legal obligation, not a policy preference. Stohnn's architecture meets that requirement by design. There is no combustion to eliminate because there was never any combustion in the system.

Stohnn is not a research project. It is a manufacturing company. The goal is to build systems, sell them to buyers who need them, and prove the architecture at commercial scale. Everything else follows from that.
